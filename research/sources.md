# Sources — AI-Powered SEO Content Production

**Topic:** AI-powered SEO content production for B2B SaaS
**Curated by:** Siddhant Kumar
**Last updated:** 2026-06-19

## How these experts were selected

The brief was explicit: find people who **practice what they teach**, not the first
Google results. So I deliberately avoided "Top 10 SEO experts" listicles (which are
SEO-gamed and circular) and instead selected on four hard filters:

1. **They operate, not just opine** — each person runs an agency, a tool, in-house
   growth at a known company, or publicly ranks their own sites. Evidence is listed
   per person below.
2. **They show their work** — published case studies with numbers, controlled
   experiments, or original data — not generic "AI is changing SEO" think-pieces.
3. **Recent + active** — content from roughly the last 6–12 months (this field moved
   fast; pre-2024 SEO advice is largely stale post-AI-Overviews).
4. **No grifters** — the AI-SEO space is full of "$10k/mo while you sleep" accounts.
   Those were filtered out. (One borderline case is documented in `/research/other/`
   as a deliberate "practitioner vs. packaging" contrast.)

The 10 below span the full production pipeline: **scaling** (Ward, Jordan),
**strategy/data** (Indig, Schwartz, Shepard), **content frameworks/optimization**
(Huang, Gübür, Hufford), and **AI-search / distribution** (Solís, Breton).
