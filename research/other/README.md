# Other materials

Supporting research that doesn't fit the LinkedIn or YouTube buckets:

- **`newsletters-and-articles.md`** — curated links to each expert's strongest recent
  *written* work (newsletter issues, blog case studies, data studies), with a short
  annotation on why each piece matters. Links + takeaways rather than full reproductions,
  to respect the authors' copyright while keeping the signal.
- **`practitioner-vs-packaging.md`** — a deliberate contrast case (Julian Goldie): a
  real operator whose *marketing* is exactly the "$10k/mo while you sleep" packaging the
  brief warned against. Kept as a judgment exercise in telling signal from sales.
